Speakers

Tamara Hadeed

Tamara is a Graphic Artist passionate about character design and traditional animation. Her body of work is full of powerful women, colorful environments and abstract storytelling. Over the years worked she has worked in many creative fields as: 2D animation for TV, creative agencies and also as a Creative director for WIZZWEAR. She share some awards with incredible people; a Latin Grammy Award (2007) for best childrens video where she participated as Art Direction assistant and colorized all scenes; and 2 Ojo de Iberoamerica (2009) awards bronze and silver for a Chevrolet Transformer campaign in which she participated as Art Director and Designer.

Alec Mendez

Alec Méndez is a Graphic Artist, Social Communicator and Multidisciplinary Creative Director based in Caracas, Venezuela. Founder and Editor in chief of the Latin American design and trends portal Wearepicta.com, Alec have been part of some of the most interesting creative projects in Venezuela, among which are: the Simpl3 audiovisual collective, the event "Through the middle of the street "and the Switch entrepreneurs meet.

Michael Wong

Visual Communicator born in Caracas, Venezuela. A former student of Prodiseño Visual Communication & Design school. His work is focused on Calligraphy, Typography, Illustration, Editorial Design, Graffiti, and skateboarding.
